Payments

To set your tuition payments up, please click the appropriate Pay Now button. This will set up a re-occuring payment on the 1st of each month through paypal. To cancel your tuition, you can stop the re-occuring payment through paypal or you can email us at laketravisjjc@gmail.com and we'll stop it for you.
