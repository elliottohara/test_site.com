Summer Camps

Lake Travis Jiu Jitsu Club is proud to announce its summer camp schedule.

Kids Martial Arts Fun Camp

Camp 1: June 15th - June 18th
Camp 2: Jun 29th - July 2nd

Our Martial Arts Fun Camps are a drop off camp in which children are closely supervised by our highly qualified staff and taught basic martial arts skills in the form of easy to understand games. No martial arts experience is needed, just the desire to have a lot of fun!

Each camp is divided into 2 sessions. Session one is 9am-12am, Mon-Thu. Session two is 1pm-4pm, Mon-Thu. Each session costs $175. Both sessions can be purchased for $325, children who come to both sessions stay through the noon hour for a full day camp. Current members of LTJJC also receive a 10% discount.

Fitness Boot Camp

Camp 1: June 22 - 25
Camp 2: July 20 - 23

LTJJC's Fitness Boot Camp will be taught by Heather O'Hara, a former Radio city Rockette, who is also certified as a personal trainer, allegro pilates and dance instructor. She uses a combination of strength, plyometrics, core, cardio, muscle endurance, flexibility and functional movement patterns to give you the ultimate circuit workout. You get EVERYTHING you need!

We use jump ropes, medicine balls, boxing, yoga-pilates, core strengthening, upper-lower body exercises and more! great for people show want that summer weight loss or athletes who want to increase agility, balance and power.

We promise you, this camp won't disappoint.
