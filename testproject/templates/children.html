Kids BJJ

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is an amazing sport for kids of all ages. Besides the obvious physical benefits of the physical activity over playing minecraft on an iPad, BJJ teaches kids many valuable life lessons. Learning techniques teaches discipline. Sparring with an opponent teaches a child respect, not only for others, but also for himself. BJJ helps children who have difficulty focusing to apply themselves, since it's difficult to let your mind wonder when you're sparring. It teaches them that acting out of frustration or anger almost never accomplishes anything beneficial.
