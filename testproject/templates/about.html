About Lake Travis Jiu-Jitsu Club

Lake Travis Jiu-Jitsu club is a martial arts facility that instills the love of fitness and martial arts to people of all ages. Our facility is located at 13908 Hwy 71 West, right across from the field of dreams on Highway 71.

We have nightly classes in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, for children and adults, Mixed Martial arts, and Cardio Kickboxing. Come check us out!
